Note of the Secretary General

Dear participants and dear Bizalmun'24 team, I am honored to present the second edition of the Bizalmun conference with this letter. When my Mun adventure first started, I was linguistically inadequate, ignorant and shy. But since my first experience, my dream has always been to be in this position one day. Maybe I'm good enough right now, maybe not, but being at this level makes me very happy and excited. After my difficult Mun experiences, I would like to say that Bizalmun'24 will be an extremely useful and trouble-free MUN for first timers or those who think their academic knowledge is not sufficient. We want to provide an environment for everyone, whether experienced or inexperienced, to have a comfortable and successful experience by taking them under our wing academically. I hope that bizalmun'24 will be a space where everyone can learn something, share their thoughts freely, and finally have a pleasant time.

Sincerely,

Elfin Ezel Tekin
