HCC: Cabinet of Nikita Khruschev

Agenda : Open agenda.

The Cabinet of Nikita Khrushchev committee invites participants to step into the heart of the Cold War, a period defined by intense geopolitical tensions and rapid change. Under Khrushchev’s leadership, the Soviet Union faced the challenge of asserting its global influence while navigating the complexities of a post-Stalinist society.

As members of Khrushchev’s inner circle, delegates will engage in high-stakes decision-making, shaping the course of history through their strategic and diplomatic choices. This committee offers a deep dive into Soviet governance, the balance of power during the Cold War, and the ideological battles that defined the era. Your decisions will influence not only the fate of the Soviet Union but also the global order of the 20th century.
